About Rachel Garcia

I am an author of three fantasy novels within the Amidst The Shadows Trilogy series and as well have authored a historical fantasy novella, The Eternal Bargain. These works include characters, tones, and plotlines of grief, witches, demons, humans, magic, chaos, love, morally grey characters, and so much more.

My first book, The Eternal Bargain, was my brave foray into the author world. I have been telling stories ever since I was a child, and writing has always been more than a hobby. It is something so profoundly myself, and is a passion I am so proud of. My writing style centers on complex plots and flawed characters that reflect the nuances of humanity.

When I’m not writing, I am binge watching a TV show, browsing used bookstores, or getting a sweet treat.
