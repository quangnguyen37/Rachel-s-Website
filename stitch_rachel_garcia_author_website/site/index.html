About the author

Meet Rachel Garcia

Rachel Garcia is a dark fantasy and fiction author. She is the author of three fantasy novels in the Amidst the Shadows trilogy and the historical fantasy novella The Eternal Bargain.

Her work explores grief, witches, demons, humans, magic, chaos, love, and morally grey characters.
