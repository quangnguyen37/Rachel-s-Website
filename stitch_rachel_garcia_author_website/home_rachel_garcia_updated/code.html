ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Meet Rachel Garcia

I am a dark fantasy and fiction author of three fantasy novels within the Amidst The Shadows Trilogy series and as well have authored a historical fiction novella, The Eternal Bargain. These works include characters, tones, and plotlines of grief, witches, demons, humans, magic, chaos, love, morally grey characters, and so much more.
