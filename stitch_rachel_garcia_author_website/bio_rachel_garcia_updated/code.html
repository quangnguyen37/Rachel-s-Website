The Storyteller

Architect of Shadows & Paper Realities.

I am an author of three fantasy novels within the Amidst The Shadows Trilogy series and as well have authored a historical fantasy novella, The Eternal Bargain.

The Craft

My writing style centers on complex plots and flawed characters that reflect the nuances of humanity. Each narrative is a tapestry woven from the threads of internal struggle and external mystery.

When I’m not writing, I am binge watching a TV show, browsing used bookstores, or getting a sweet treat. I believe the best stories are found in the quiet, everyday moments just as much as the grand epics.
